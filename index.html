About Me

I am a PhD Candidate studying data practices of advocacy organization with the CU Boulder Department of Information Science I am advised by Amy Voida in the Philanthropic Informatics Research Lab (PIRL).
My current research interests include:

  • Data Feminist Practices in Advocacy
  • Critical Data Science: Data in Electoral Communication
  • Interorganizational Data Infrastructures for Social Good

    • I have experience as a union organizer, a think tank researcher, and as a refugee caseworker. I also have dual BAs in philosophy and international politics from Penn State University.
